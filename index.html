About me

I am a results-driven Senior Core Network Engineer with extensive experience in mobile core network operations, design, deployment, troubleshooting, and optimization across 2G, 4G/LTE, and 5G networks. Strong expertise in CS Core, PS Core, EPC, 5G Core (5GC), NFV, and MVNO platforms, delivering high-availability, scalable, and secure telecommunications solutions.

Hands-on experience in core network architecture, capacity planning, network integration, performance optimization, fault management, and root cause analysis. Proven ability to design and implement automation, monitoring, and alerting systems that improve network stability, latency, throughput, and SLA compliance.

I have extensive hands-on experience with a wide range of telecom signaling and transport protocols, combined with strong troubleshooting skills across circuit-switched and packet-switched networks. My expertise includes SS7 (ISUP, SCCP, MAP, CAP), SIP, Diameter, SIGTRAN (M3UA, SCCP), H.248, RTP/RTCP, MGCP, and BICC, as well as IP and transport protocols such as TCP/IP, UDP, DNS, DHCP, SBI and MPLS. I have worked on call setup and teardown analysis, routing and interconnection issues, 2G/4G/5G signaling flows, call forwarding and supplementary services, roaming, and interoperability troubleshooting between multi-vendor core network elements including MSC, GMSC, SBC, MME, HSS, EPC and 5GC platforms.

Above and beyond, I have strong expertise in SS7 security, with hands-on experience in identifying, analyzing, and mitigating signaling vulnerabilities that impact subscriber privacy and network integrity. My skills include detecting and preventing SS7-based attacks such as location tracking, call and SMS interception, unauthorized call forwarding, and subscriber impersonation through MAP, CAP, and ISUP message abuse. I have worked on SS7 firewall configuration, signaling traffic analysis, and anomaly detection to identify malformed or suspicious messages, enforce global title screening, and apply filtering rules based on operator trust models. This experience enables me to proactively assess network exposure, strengthen interconnection security, and ensure compliance with industry security best practices.

Technically strong professional with deep knowledge of LTE/EPC, 5G SA/NSA, NFV/SDN, cloud-native core networks, and telecom security systems. Recently, involved actively in hands-on lab deployments, testing, and system integrations using open-source telecom platforms such as Open5GS, Kamailio, FreeSWITCH, and freeDiameter to validate core network signaling, Diameter routing, SIP/VoIP services, and 5G core workflows. Experienced in building test environments, performing interoperability testing, and integrating open-source components with commercial network elements.

I am recognized for strong problem-solving, project execution, and risk mitigation skills, with a commitment to continuous learning, innovation, and operational excellence in fast-paced telecommunications environments.
